Hummus Avocado Toast

5 mins | 🌱 Vegan

527 kcal

Carbs (45g) 48%
Fat (30g) 32%
Protein (19g) 20%
Customize Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 slices Whole Wheat Bread
  • 1/2 Avocado, mashed
  • 2 tbsp Hummus
  • Pinch of salt, pepper, and chili flakes

Instructions

  1. Toast the slices of bread to your desired crunchiness.
  2. Spread 1 tablespoon of hummus evenly on each slice of toast.
  3. Top with mashed avocado.
  4. Sprinkle with salt, pepper, and a pinch of chili flakes before serving.
